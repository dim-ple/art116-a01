About the Owner

Alex Greene is a passionate and experienced bookkeeper with a focus on the real estate industry.
As the owner of Keep a Green Book, LLC, Alex has dedicated herself to helping clients manage their finances with precision and care.

With a background in Real Estate, New Construction, and Renovation, Alex has a deep understanding of the unique financial
needs of these industries. Her expertise allows her to provide customized solutions that ensure her clients' financial success.

Alex's attention to detail and commitment to accuracy have earned her a reputation as a trustworthy and reliable bookkeeper.
She takes pride in delivering exceptional service that exceeds her clients' expectations.

When she's not crunching numbers, Alex enjoys exploring the outdoors and spending time with her family.
She is dedicated to environmental causes and works hard to minimize her company's impact on the planet.
